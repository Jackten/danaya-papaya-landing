The training principle

Tear.
Repair.
Come back stronger.

Not fake-it-till-you-make-it. Not a new personality. Small, brave acts repeated until your nervous system learns: I can do this.

My starting point

A wallflower from the Soviet Union.

I am not innately confident. I grew up learning to stay small, follow the expected path, and not attract too much attention. I am deeply suspicious of any blueprint that claims one version of confidence fits everyone.

Where I am now

Now I do stand-up.

I still stutter when there are more than a dozen people in the room — even if five of them performed before me and another five will go after. Courage did not erase the fear. It taught me that fear does not get the final vote.

I will not ask you to become fearless. I will help you move while fear is still talking.

What we can work on

  • Dating profile and app cleanup — photos, prompts, messages, pacing.
  • Meeting people offline — venues, introductions, routines, follow-through.
  • Scripts that sound like you — invitations, boundaries, honest conversations.
  • Weekly accountability — actions, debriefs, and course correction.

What this is not

  • No tricks, pressure tactics, or status games.
  • No pretending rejection does not sting.
  • No generic dating persona pasted over your actual character.
  • No blaming every outcome on yourself — or everyone else.
