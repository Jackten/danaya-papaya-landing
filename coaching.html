Meet Danaya

I’m a wallflower turned sunflower.I’m
a wallflower

I grew up in post-Soviet Kazakhstan and moved through six or eight different schools. Social ease did not come intuitively to me. I learnt by watching closely, noticing patterns, trying, repairing and trying again.

I processed emotions logically before they became intuitive. After enough real-life reps in protecting my peace, setting boundaries and choosing joy, what I once had to calculate began to feel natural.

What I learnt consciously, I can now explain consciously.

The work is rational and compassionate. Honest without cruelty. Serious — but never so solemn that you and I cannot laugh at the absurdity of being human.
